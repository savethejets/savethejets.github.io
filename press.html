Description

3..2..1..Grenades! is designed to bring back fun memories of great games like Goldeneye. The game is aimed at first person shooter players of any skill level.

History

The game started as a week long game jam. Deemed #FullIndieJam by it's creator Kyle Reczek. The resulting game was demoed at the Full Indie Bash event in Calgary, AB and was a hit with the crowds. Full time development was started soon after that. The game will debut on itch.io and hopefully with some luck get on Steam.
