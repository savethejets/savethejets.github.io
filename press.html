Description

3..2..1..Grenades! is designed to bring back fun memories of great games like Goldeneye. The game is aimed at first person shooter players of any skill level.

History

Kyle Reczek has been making games in one form or another for many years. He first got started with level editing, working on one of the first mods for Red Orchestra: Mare Nostrum. Later he went to school for Software Development and began making PC games. 3..2..1..Grenades will be his first Steam release. It was created in a week long game jam, and debuted at Indie Game Bash.
