Description

3..2..1..Grenades 3..2..1..Grenades! Is a snowball fight with high explosives! The game is aimed at first person shooter players of any skill level. Play in an adventure mode to help save Grenades Co from the alien monster they've unleashed! by yourself or with up to 4 friends

Key Features:

  • Adventure mode gameplay, help Grenades Co fight back the alien menace it has unleashed with yourself and up to 4 friends.
  • Over 20 levels ranging from waterfalls to alien planets
  • Lots of multiplayer modes including: Deathmatch, Fallball, Tennis, Capture the Pug, Paintball, Target practice
  • 20 Extra Mix-and-Matchable Game Modes including: Massive explosions, Big heads, Invisible grenades, Slo(ooo)w Motion fights, Low Gravity, Turbo Speed, Bullseye only, Many more!

History

3..2..1..Grenades started as a week long jam game. My main goal was to create a first person shooter that was accessible to everyone. Not just professional players who have mastered pinpoint accuracy. So the first thing I did was throw out shooting! Everyone understands throwing things. It's built into all of our human experiences. so I felt like that was a good place to start design wise. Grenades in most games are also usually just used as secondary weapons, I felt like they needed their time in the sun! Also I've always had a love for games like Diddy-Kong Racing and wanted to incorporate things that were interesting about the mash up of the adventure genre with the racing genre into a first person shooter.
